About

Nt00 radio | outside the algorithm

What it is

Nt00 radio is a freeform radio space where anyone can play the music they like, built around diverse music, discovery, and sharing good music. With a focus on underground global sounds.

Why it exists

The station exists as a small response to passive, algorithmic consumption culture. Instead of serving predictable recommendations, it creates room for music exploration and unexpected discoveries.

What to expect

  • Live sets, 24/7 stream with rotating selections and curated programming
  • Hip hop, jungle, Crankwave, drum & bass, dubstep, experimental, dub, punk, metal and literally anything that makes us feel.

For more music:

Follow the station on Instagram, YouTube, and SoundCloud for updates, archives, and related material.

You want to contribute?

If you like this project and love music, you're welcome to play some music, write about your favorite band or DJ, make a playlist, or share anything music-related. Contact us at nt00radio@protonmail.com or on Instagram.
