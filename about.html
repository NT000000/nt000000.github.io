About

Freeform radio outside the algorithm

What it is

Escape The Algorithm is a freeform radio space built around discovery and sharing good music.

With a focus on underground global sounds and diverse music.

Why it exists

The station exists as a small response to passive consumption algorithmic culture. Instead of serving predictable recommendations, it creates room for music exploration and unexpected discoveries.

What to expect

  • Live sets, 24/7 stream with rotating selections and curated programming
  • Hip hop, jungle, Crankwave, drum & bass, dubstep, experimental, dub, punk, metal and literally anything that makes us feel.

Follow the station on Instagram, YouTube, and SoundCloud for updates, archives, and related material.
